updateState(item, currentDate, shelfLife)

Updates the state of an item object based on its expiry date and shelf life It's new if it's not expired and has 0 checks (checks happen once a week) It's expired if its expiry date is less than the current date It's old if its checks are greater than the shelf life of an item Otherwise it's considered valid
Parameters:
